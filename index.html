Privacy Policy — Lone Star Family Care App

Effective Date: April 2026  |  Last Updated: April 2026

Overview

Lone Star Family Care operates the Lone Star Family Care iOS application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our App. The App does not collect, transmit, or share your personal information.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not:

  • Collect your name, email, phone number, or any personal identifiers
  • Require account creation or login
  • Track your location
  • Use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking
  • Transmit any data to our servers or any third-party server
  • Share any information with third parties

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The App includes optional health tools. Any data you enter is stored only on your device using iOS standard local storage (UserDefaults/AppStorage). This data never leaves your device, can be cleared within the App, and is permanently deleted when you uninstall the App.

  • Hydration Tracker: Daily glass count, goal, and up to 7 days of history — stored locally only.
  • Blood Pressure Log: Readings including systolic, diastolic, pulse, date, and notes — stored locally only. Share readings with your provider directly.
  • BMI Calculator: No data stored. Calculations are in-memory only.

Third-Party Services

The App links to external services. Their respective privacy policies apply.

  • Athena Health (Scheduling & Portal): Opens via Apple's in-app browser (SFSafariViewController). Governed by Athena Health's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Maps: "Get Directions" opens Apple Maps, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Phone and Email: One-tap links open your device's native Phone and Mail apps. No data passes through our App.
  • Social Media: TikTok and Instagram links open in Safari, subject to those platforms' policies.

Medical Disclaimer

The BMI Calculator, Hydration Tracker, and Blood Pressure Log are for personal tracking and informational purposes only. They are not medical devices and do not provide medical advice. Blood pressure categories reflect American Heart Association (AHA) guidelines. Always consult your healthcare provider regarding your health data and any symptoms you experience.

Children's Privacy

The App serves patients ages 5 and up and their caregivers. No personal information is collected from anyone, including minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The Last Updated date will reflect any changes.
