Privacy Policy — Lone Star Family Care App

Effective Date: April 2026  |  Last Updated: April 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Lone Star Family Care handles information in connection with the Lone Star Family Care iOS application (the “App”). Based on the current version of the App, Lone Star Family Care does not collect personal information through the App, does not require users to create an account, and does not store user-entered health-tool data on Lone Star Family Care servers.

Information the App Does Not Collect

Based on the current version of the App, the App does not itself:

  • Require account creation or login
  • Collect your name, email address, phone number, or similar identifiers through the App
  • Track your precise location
  • Use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking technologies within the App
  • Store user-entered health-tool data on Lone Star Family Care servers
  • Sell personal information

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The App includes optional tools that allow you to enter information for personal reference. Information entered into these tools is stored locally on your device using standard iOS local storage mechanisms. Lone Star Family Care does not receive this locally stored information through the App.

  • Hydration Tracker: Daily glass count, goal, and limited recent history may be stored locally on your device.
  • Blood Pressure Log: Readings such as systolic, diastolic, pulse, date, and notes may be stored locally on your device.
  • BMI Calculator: Calculations are performed within the App. Information is not intended to be transmitted to Lone Star Family Care through the App.

You can remove locally stored information by clearing it within the App where that option is available, or by deleting the App from your device. Device backups and device-level settings are controlled by Apple and your personal device configuration, not by Lone Star Family Care.

Third-Party Services and External Links

The App may provide links to, or open, third-party services and device-native applications. When you leave the App or open an external service, that third party’s terms, policies, and privacy practices apply. Lone Star Family Care is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

  • Athenahealth Scheduling and Patient Portal: The App may open Athenahealth pages in Apple’s in-app browser. Use of those services is governed by Athenahealth’s terms and privacy practices. Athenahealth Privacy Policy
  • Apple Maps: Directions may open in Apple Maps. Apple Privacy Policy
  • Phone and Mail: Tapping phone or email links may open your device’s native apps, which are subject to Apple’s and your device provider’s settings and policies.
  • Web Browser and Social Media: Website, Instagram, TikTok, and similar links may open in Safari or another browser, where those third-party services’ privacy practices apply.

Medical Disclaimer

The BMI Calculator, Hydration Tracker, Blood Pressure Log, and any related wellness or informational content in the App are provided for general informational and personal tracking purposes only. They are not medical devices, are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or condition, and do not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Always seek the advice of a qualified healthcare professional regarding any medical condition, symptoms, readings, or treatment decisions. If you believe you are experiencing a medical emergency, call 911 or seek emergency medical care immediately.

Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for general patient and caregiver use. Lone Star Family Care does not knowingly collect personal information from children through the App.

Data Security

Information entered into the App’s optional local tools is stored on your device. The security of that information depends in part on the security of your device, operating system, passcode, backup settings, and other device-level protections. While Lone Star Family Care aims to minimize data handling within the App, no method of electronic storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

Lone Star Family Care may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last Updated” date above. Your continued use of the App after an updated Privacy Policy is posted indicates your acceptance of the revised policy.
